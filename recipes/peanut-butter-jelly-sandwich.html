Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich (PB&J)

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 1 tbsp Grape Jelly
  • 2 tbsp Peanut Butter

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Combine the slices with the peanut butter and jelly in the middle.
  4. Enjoy!
