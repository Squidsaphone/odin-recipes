Oatmeal

Image Credit: Mart Production


Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Oats
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tbsp Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • .25 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Pour milk and salt into a pot and stir.
  2. Bring milk to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Add oats to pot and stir.
  4. Let boil for 4 minutes.
  5. Add maple syrup and cinnamon to pot and stir.
  6. Boil for 1 minute longer.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
