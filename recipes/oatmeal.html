Oatmeal

Image Credit: Mart Production


Description

A classic breakfast staple, this oatmeal is both easy to make and very tasty. Taking only 5 minutes to prepare, this simple dish is a great choice for a busy morning rush. Enhanced by the addition of cinnamon and maple syrup, this oatmeal goes beyond your standard oat. Pair with your favorite fruit juice and a slice of buttered toast to start your day off right.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Oats
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tbsp Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • .25 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Pour milk and salt into a pot and stir.
  2. Bring milk to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Add oats to pot and stir.
  4. Let boil for 4 minutes.
  5. Add maple syrup and cinnamon to pot and stir.
  6. Boil for 1 minute longer.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
