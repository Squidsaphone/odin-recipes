Description

This dish is a simple bowl of Raisin Bran cereal. Combining store-bought Raisin Bran and your milk of choice makes an incredibly simple, quick, and delicious breakfast. Pair with sliced fruit, scrambled egg/tofu, and a refreshing glass of orange juice for a great start to any morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Raisin Bran Cereal
  • 1 cup Milk

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into bowl.
  2. Pour milk into bowl.
  3. Enjoy!
